About Me

About my work

My name is Drew. I am a web developer, I can do anything HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Recently I’ve fallen in love with Sass and Angular 4. I have posted some of my projects online. am currently learning React and I am dabbling with Python. My current project is called Head Coach, it’s a football based RPG, still underdevelopment, but perhaps it's the only one of its kind on the web! You can see some of my other projects on my project section..

A little about me...

My family and I currently reside in Fairbanks, AK, we are planning to move to the SLC Valley towards the end of the summer. We enjoy all the outdoor activities Utah has to offer. Personally, I am into sports, namely football, I am a huge Carolina Panthers fan and SEC football in the college ranks. Oh yes, I drink lots of coffee (check out Bones Coffee!), one may say I am an addict, but that’s debate able.
